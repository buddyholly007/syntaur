Accuracy tips

The scanner uses AI vision to read your documents. For best results:

• Upload clear, high-resolution scans (photos of paper documents work too)

• PDFs are automatically converted to high-res images for better reading

• If you see frequent errors, switching to a more capable model in Settings can significantly improve accuracy. Recommended models for document scanning:

• Anthropic Claude Sonnet — best accuracy for tax documents

• OpenAI GPT-4o — excellent OCR accuracy

• Google Gemini 2.0 Flash — good accuracy, free on OpenRouter

• Qwen2 VL 72B (free on OpenRouter) — strong vision, zero cost

• Always verify extracted values against your original documents
